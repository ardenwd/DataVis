For the past few years Charli XCX has been one of my top artists.

I mostly listed to Nashville's pop radio stations growing up and at 13 years old when I first heard her songs, I didn't even know they were hers.

When I was introduced to Charli's self-titled album I was hooked.

Let's explore what this album is made of.

There are 15 songs on the album.

Some songs have artists featured, while others do not.

And the lyrics are all over the place.
